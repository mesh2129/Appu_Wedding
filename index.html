How We Met

I'm overwhelmed with happiness as I think about our journey. We met through a mutual friend while I was jogging. He was a familiar face from school, but he barely noticed me then. Fast forward to the lockdown, we started talking, and our daily conversations became my comfort. We'd talk every evening, sharing stories and laughter. One day, I confessed my feelings, and to my surprise, he felt the same. That midnight, he called and said 'I like you too.' Tears of joy filled my eyes, and our love story began. We faced challenges, but our love grew stronger. We cherished every moment – bike rides, talks, and even late-night calls. But then he flew abroad and I was left in chaos missing his presence.Everyday felt empty without him. Yet,our love endured and we found ways to bridge the distance. Now, after four years, we're here, ready to start our new life.
